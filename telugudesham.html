TELUGUDESHAM



President : Chandrababu Naidu

Telugu Desam Party or TEDP is a national political party in India . Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao , the founder of the Telugu Desam Party and the idol of the Telugu people, launched it on March 29 , 1982 . [1] He founded it with the ambition of having a regional party as an alternative to the Congress party which was ruling the state unilaterally . After establishing the party, he became a monk and vowed to dedicate his life to the Telugu people and the restoration of the self-respect of the Telugu race. In the 13th Lok Sabha (1999-2004), it became the fourth largest party with 29 members.
